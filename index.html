The History Of The Black Battalion

The Black Battalion was formed in Pictou, Nova Scotia. It was the first black battalion in Canadian history. Recruitment took place across Canada but most signed up from the Maritimes. It was difficult for people of colour to enlist because of the racism they had to face by the most people who were in charge of military enlistment.
